Cranberry Brie Bites

These cranberry Brie bites are an appetizer that will surely wow your guests at any party or holiday function. Tangy cranberry, creamy Brie, and crunchy walnuts come together in a buttery puff pastry shell, delivering the perfect balance of flavor and texture in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 8-ounce round of Brie
  • Puff pastry
  • Cranberry sauce
  • Walnuts
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Roll: Roll the puff pastry into a rectangle.
  2. Cut: Place your Brie in the freezer for about 20 minutes to make it easier to cut. Meanwhile, use a sharp knife to cut the puff pastry into even squares. Remove the Brie from the freezer and cut it into even pieces.
  3. Assemble: Press the pastry squares into an oiled muffin tin. Spoon each square with a teaspoon of cranberry sauce, then top with the Brie. Sprinkle with walnuts and a pinch of sea salt.
  4. Chill: Transfer the cranberry Brie bites to the fridge and chill for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown.
